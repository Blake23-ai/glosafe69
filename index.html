Customize Your Arm Cuff

Materials

Our GloSafe arm cuffs are made from durable glow-in-the-dark TPU material. TPU provides flexibility, strength, and long-lasting glow performance, ensuring the band stays lightweight and comfortable while offering high visibility in dark environments. The embroidery option will use black stickers created on a Cricut machine.

The Cuff's Purpose

Our glow-in-the-dark arm bands are designed to ensure safety for humans while performing daily tasks where darkness increases the risk of injury. These stylish bands are fully customizable in color, size, shape, and embroidery to guarantee maximum satisfaction for each customer. GloSafe's inventory arm cuffs are ideal for anyone concerned with home and nighttime safety, such as parents and guardians, as well as those needing visibility while exercising—like athletes and trainers—when light is limited.
